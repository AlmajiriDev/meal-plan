Foods & Swaps

✅ Safe Foods (Multi-Condition Plan)

  • Carbs: Pap (ogi), oatmeal, millet porridge, white rice, boiled yam, plantain, sweet potato
  • Proteins: Fresh fish (boiled/steamed), lean chicken (skinless), skinless turkey, eggs, tofu, beans (soft)
  • Iron-rich additions: Lean red meat (small, well-cooked soft), beef/liver (1×/week), ugwu, ewedu, spinach (small amounts)
  • Vegetables: Carrots, green beans, okra, pumpkin leaves (ugwu), ewedu, scent leaf
  • Fruits: Ripe banana (best for iron + sleep), ripe pawpaw (small), honeydew melon, watermelon (chewed well)
  • Liquids: Warm water, plain soya milk, plain yogurt (test tolerance — dairy can worsen GERD/bile reflux)
  • Sleep-promoting evening: Warm soya milk + small honey, banana, sweet potato

Fat rule: ≤ ½ tsp oil per meal TOTAL. Olive oil preferred over palm oil. No frying. Steam/boil/grill only.

❌ Avoid Strictly (All Conditions)

  • Pepper/chili (fresh or dry)
  • Onions, garlic, ginger (PUD)
  • Maggi, Knorr, Royco, all stock cubes
  • Tomatoes (fresh or paste), vinegar
  • Citrus (orange, lemon, lime, grapefruit)
  • Carbonated drinks (Coke, Fanta, Sprite)
  • Alcohol, smoking, cigarettes
  • Chocolate, mint (triggers GERD)
  • Fried foods (GERD + bile reflux)
  • Coffee, strong tea (PUD + insomnia)
  • Smoked fish, dried meat, agbo/herbal mixtures

Critical: If you have bile reflux, AVOID all fats above ½ tsp/meal. Long gaps between meals WORSE bile reflux — never skip a meal.

🌧️ Cold/Rainy Day Swaps

  • Cold pap → Hot pap with honey
  • Bread + water → Bread + hot soya milk
  • Cold broth → Hot broth with scent leaf
  • Plain rice → Rice soaked in hot broth
  • Cold fruit → Gently warmed pawpaw puree

🔄 Missing Ingredient Swaps

  • Fish → Chicken or 2 boiled eggs
  • Eggs → Tofu (mashed) or soft beans
  • Yam → Plantain or sweet potato
  • Carrots → Pumpkin (boiled, mashed)
  • Pap → Oatmeal or millet porridge
  • Soya milk → Warm thin pap or nono

🍌 Fruit-Only Day (Low Appetite)

  • 6:30 AM — 1 ripe banana + warm water
  • 9:30 AM — ½ ripe pawpaw
  • 1:00 PM — Rice + ½ avocado (if hungry)
  • 5:00 PM — Coconut water + banana
  • 8:00 PM — ½ honeydew OR warm soya milk + small banana (sleep-friendly)

Use for 1–2 days max when appetite is low.

Suggested Substitutions (Per Condition)

🩸 Iron-Boosting Alternatives

Swap these into your week to fight iron deficiency. Always cook soft & plain — no pepper.

  • Liver (1×/week, small): Boiled & mashed — mix into rice or eat with bread. Highest iron.
  • Lean beef (2×/week): Soft-boiled with carrot & a tiny bit of olive oil. Strip any fat.
  • Ugwu/Ewedu/Spinach: Twice this week — pureed smooth, no pepper, tiny oil.
  • Beans: Already in plan (moin-moin) — keep 2–3×/week.
  • Eggs: Already daily — yolk has iron.
  • Cooked carrots: Already in plan — iron content modest but helps.

Iron absorption tip: Eat iron foods WITH a small piece of lean meat in the same meal — the "meat factor" boosts absorption 2–3×. (Vitamin C would help more but it's off-limits for ulcer.)

😴 Sleep-Promoting Substitutions

Use these for evening & nightcap meals when sleep is hard:

  • Sweet potato (dinner): Boiled, mashed with tiny salt. Tryptophan precursor — helps melatonin.
  • Warm soya milk + small honey: 30 min before bed — natural tryptophan.
  • Banana: Has magnesium + tryptophan. Last fruit of the day.
  • Chicken or turkey: Tryptophan-rich — good for afternoon meal.
  • Eggs: Also tryptophan-rich — good any meal.
  • Cooked ugwu/ewedu: Magnesium — helps relaxation.

Sleep hygiene: No caffeine after noon. No screens 1 hr before bed. Same bedtime daily. Cool, dark bedroom.

💛 Bile Reflux — Critical Substitutions

  • Reduce fat to ZERO if flaring — even ½ tsp oil can trigger. Use broth-only days.
  • Broth-only reset (1 day): Chicken broth + plain rice + banana. Then re-introduce carefully.
  • Lean protein: All visible fat removed. Skin off chicken. Fish — only the white meat (no oily fish).
  • No "missing" oil: If a recipe needs oil, swap with extra broth.
  • Eat within 1 hr of waking: Critical for bile flow regulation.
  • Last meal 3+ hr before bed: Non-negotiable.
Condition-Specific Guidance

🔥 If You Have GERD

  • Strictly NO food 3+ hours before bed — non-negotiable
  • Sleep on your LEFT side — anatomy reduces acid exposure
  • Head of bed elevated 6–8 inches (extra pillow or wedge)
  • Don't bend over after eating — keeps acid in stomach
  • Avoid tight clothing around waist
  • Chew gum (sugar-free) after meals — increases saliva, neutralizes acid
  • Stop eating at 80% full — extra critical for you

💛 If You Have Bile Reflux

  • NEVER skip meals — including breakfast. Hunger makes bile build up
  • Limit fat to < 30g/day TOTAL — fat triggers bile release
  • Eat 5 small meals (already in plan) — keeps bile flowing gently
  • Avoid large meals — even one big meal can trigger a flare
  • If you vomit, yellow/green color = bile — note for next doctor visit
  • Combine iron food with a small portion of meat in the SAME meal — boosts iron absorption (meat factor)

🩸 If You Have Iron Deficiency

  • Talk to your doctor about iron tablets. Diet alone usually isn't enough at your level
  • Take iron pills on an empty stomach with water (if tolerated) for best absorption
  • Avoid tea/coffee within 1 hour of iron pills (tannins block absorption)
  • Eat iron-rich foods WITH a small piece of meat in the same meal (meat factor)
  • Best iron sources you can have: liver (1×/week), lean red meat (soft-cooked), ugwu, ewedu, beans, eggs
  • Note: vitamin C (citrus) would help absorption BUT it's forbidden due to ulcer — so we rely on meat factor
  • Cook in cast iron pots if possible — adds small iron to food
  • Get a hemoglobin test every 2–3 months to track recovery

😴 If You Have Insomnia

  • No caffeine after noon (even tea, cocoa)
  • No screens 1 hour before bed (use night mode if you must)
  • Final meal of day: try warm soya milk + small honey + banana (tryptophan + complex carb)
  • Sweet potato at dinner is sleep-promoting (tryptophan precursor)
  • Sleep schedule: same bedtime/wake time every day, even weekends
  • Keep bedroom cool (18–20°C), dark, quiet
  • If awake > 20 min, get up & read in dim light — don't stay in bed frustrated
  • Magnesium-rich foods (ugwu, ewedu, banana, beans) help sleep
  • Avoid napping after 3 PM if it affects night sleep
Critical Reminders
🚨 Go to hospital if you have:

• Black, tarry stool (digestive bleeding)
• Vomiting blood or "coffee grounds"
• Vomiting yellow/green fluid repeatedly (bile reflux flare)
• Severe pain that won't stop
• Sudden weight loss >3kg/month
• Difficulty swallowing (food sticks in chest)
• Chest pain that mimics a heart attack
• Yellow skin or eyes (jaundice — possible bile duct issue)
• Extreme fatigue + pale skin + shortness of breath (severe anemia)
• Persistent insomnia despite following the plan for 2+ weeks

💊 Medical Rules (All Conditions)

  • Take all prescribed meds on time (PPIs, antacids, iron, etc.)
  • Get tested for H. pylori — if positive, complete triple/quadruple therapy
  • No NSAIDs (ibuprofen, diclofenac) — paracetamol only for pain
  • No smoking, no alcohol, no agbo/herbal mixtures
  • See a gastroenterologist — your condition stack (PUD + GERD + bile reflux + anemia + insomnia) needs specialist care, not just diet
  • Review with doctor every 2 weeks during recovery
  • Get a full blood count (FBC) every 2–3 months to track iron/B12 levels

🍽️ Daily Eating Rules (Multi-Condition)

  • Chewing: Every bite 20–30 times until liquid
  • Never skip meals — critical for bile reflux
  • Stop at 80% full — never finish the plate
  • Eat freshly cooked food only — no leftovers
  • No water 30 min before or 1 hour after meals
  • Sip 6–8 glasses of warm water daily (between meals)
  • Eat fruit alone, never with other food (digestion clash)
  • Fruit must be ripe, room temperature
  • Sleep position: LEFT side, head raised 6–8"
  • Last food of day ≥ 3 hours before bed (strict for GERD)
  • Limit fat to < ½ tsp oil per meal TOTAL — no frying
