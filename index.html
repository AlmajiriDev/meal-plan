Foods & Swaps

✅ Safe Foods

  • Pap (ogi), oatmeal, millet porridge
  • White rice, boiled yam, plantain, sweet potato
  • Fresh fish, chicken (skinless), eggs, tofu
  • Carrots, green beans, okra, ugwu, ewedu
  • Ripe banana, pawpaw, watermelon, honeydew
  • Avocado, coconut water, plain yogurt (nono)

❌ Avoid Strictly

  • Pepper/chili (fresh or dry)
  • Onions, garlic, ginger
  • Maggi, Knorr, Royco, all cubes
  • Tomatoes (fresh or paste), vinegar
  • Citrus (orange, lemon, lime)
  • Kuli-kuli, fried foods, palm oil in excess
  • Alcohol, Coke, Fanta, Sprite, coffee
  • Smoked fish, agbo, herbal mixtures

🌧️ Cold/Rainy Day Swaps

  • Cold pap → Hot pap with honey
  • Bread + water → Bread + hot soya milk
  • Cold broth → Hot broth with scent leaf
  • Plain rice → Rice soaked in hot broth
  • Cold fruit → Gently warmed pawpaw puree

🔄 Missing Ingredient Swaps

  • Fish → Chicken or 2 boiled eggs
  • Eggs → Tofu (mashed) or soft beans
  • Yam → Plantain or sweet potato
  • Carrots → Pumpkin (boiled, mashed)
  • Pap → Oatmeal or millet porridge
  • Soya milk → Warm thin pap or nono

🍌 Fruit-Only Day (Low Appetite)

  • 6:30 AM — 1 ripe banana + warm water
  • 9:30 AM — ½ ripe pawpaw
  • 1:00 PM — Rice + ½ avocado (if hungry)
  • 5:00 PM — Coconut water + banana
  • 8:00 PM — ½ honeydew or skip

Use for 1–2 days max when appetite is low.

Critical Reminders
🚨 Go to hospital if you have:

• Black, tarry stool (bleeding)
• Vomiting blood or "coffee grounds"
• Severe pain that won't stop
• Sudden weight loss >3kg/month
• Difficulty swallowing

💊 Medical Rules

  • Take antacids as prescribed
  • Get tested for H. pylori
  • Complete triple/quadruple therapy if positive
  • No NSAIDs (ibuprofen, diclofenac) — use paracetamol only
  • No smoking or alcohol
  • No agbo or herbal mixtures
  • Review with doctor every 2 weeks
  • Sleep with head raised 6–8 inches
  • Wait 3 hours after eating before lying down

🍽️ Daily Eating Rules

  • Chew every bite 20–30 times (until liquid)
  • Stop at 80% full — never finish the plate
  • Eat freshly cooked food only — no leftovers
  • No water 30 min before or 1 hour after meals
  • Sip 6–8 glasses of warm water daily
  • Eat fruit alone, never with other food
  • Fruit must be ripe, room temperature
